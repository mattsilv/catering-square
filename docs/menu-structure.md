# Menu Structure

## Categories

### 1. Individual Meals (10 min)

**Signature Salads** (10 items, all convertible to wraps):
- Chicken Caesar, Thai Chicken Crunch, Buffalo Chicken
- Tokyo Supergreens, Mezze Crunch, Crispy Chicken Poblano
- Plant Power, Modern Greek Crunch, Balsamic Chicken Avocado
- Earth Bowl

**Wrap-Only Items** (2 items):
- Honey Crispy Chicken Wrap
- Spicy Chicken Caesar Wrap

**Bundle Option**: Add chips + cookie to any meal

### 2. Build Your Own Salad Bar (10 min)

**Selection Rules**:
- Greens: Choose 2 from 6 options
- Protein: Choose 1 from 6 options
- Cheese: Choose 1 from 6 options
- Toppings: Choose 6 from 21 options (additional available)
- Dressings: Choose 3 from 16 options (additional available)

### 3. Smoothies (no minimum)

4 varieties: Almond Berry Blast, PB Protein (30g), Strawberry Banana, Detox Cleanse

### 4. Snacks & Beverages (no minimum)

**Snacks**:
- Skinny Dipped Almonds (2 flavors)
- Chocolate Chip Cookies (individual or platter of 10)
- Assorted Chips
- Soup (2 varieties, 2 sizes)

**Beverages**: Poppi, Open Water, LaCroix, Coke, Diet Coke
